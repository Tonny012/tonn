Shipping Policy

Effective Date: May 27, 2026

Welcome to K G Finger Monkeys . We are committed to ensuring the safe, ethical, and responsible transportation of every finger monkey placed through our program.

The safety, comfort, and wellbeing of our animals remain our highest priority throughout the transportation and delivery process.

1. Shipping Availability

We may offer transportation assistance, delivery coordination, or pickup arrangements depending on the buyer’s location and applicable regulations.

  • Shipping availability may vary by state or region
  • Certain locations may prohibit primate ownership or transportation
  • All buyers are responsible for verifying local and state exotic animal laws before purchase
  • We reserve the right to decline transportation requests that may place the animal at risk

2. Animal Safety During Transportation

Every transportation arrangement is carefully planned with the animal’s health and wellbeing in mind.

  • Animals are transported in secure, climate-conscious travel environments
  • Travel schedules are selected to minimize stress and unnecessary delays
  • Transportation may be delayed if weather or safety conditions are unsuitable
  • We reserve the right to postpone shipment for the animal’s protection

We will never knowingly transport an animal in unsafe weather or environmental conditions.

3. Pickup Appointments

Local pickup may be available by appointment only.

  • Appointments must be confirmed in advance
  • Valid identification may be required during pickup
  • Remaining balances must be cleared prior to release of the animal
  • Buyers should arrive prepared with appropriate transportation accommodations

4. Shipping Timeline

Estimated transportation timelines may vary depending on:

  • Weather conditions
  • Airline or transportation schedules
  • Veterinary clearance requirements
  • Location and travel distance
  • State and local regulations
  • Animal health and readiness

While we strive to provide accurate delivery estimates, transportation schedules are not guaranteed and may occasionally change due to circumstances beyond our control.

5. Transportation Fees

Shipping or transportation costs may vary based on:

  • Destination location
  • Travel requirements
  • Carrier availability
  • Veterinary documentation
  • Climate-related travel accommodations

Transportation fees will be discussed and confirmed prior to finalizing travel arrangements.

6. Buyer Responsibilities

Buyers are responsible for:

  • Ensuring exotic primate ownership is legal in their area
  • Providing accurate delivery and contact information
  • Being available during scheduled pickup or delivery times
  • Preparing a safe and suitable environment before the animal arrives
  • Following all recommended care instructions after arrival

Failure to comply with transportation or pickup arrangements may result in delays or additional fees.

7. Delays & Unforeseen Circumstances

Delays may occasionally occur due to:

  • Weather conditions
  • Transportation restrictions
  • Airline scheduling issues
  • Veterinary concerns
  • Government or regulatory requirements
  • Unexpected travel disruptions

We are not responsible for delays caused by third-party transportation providers or circumstances outside our reasonable control.

8. Live Arrival & Health

We prioritize the safe arrival of every finger monkey and carefully prepare each animal prior to transportation.

Buyers are encouraged to inspect their animal immediately upon arrival and schedule a veterinary wellness examination within 72 hours.

Any concerns regarding the animal’s condition should be reported promptly with supporting documentation where applicable.

9. Restricted Locations

Finger monkeys (pygmy marmosets) may be restricted or prohibited in certain states, counties, or municipalities. Buyers are solely responsible for verifying applicable laws before placing a reservation or purchase.

We reserve the right to refuse shipment to any location where ownership or transportation may violate local regulations or compromise animal welfare.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Shipping Policy periodically to reflect operational, legal, or transportation changes.

Continued use of our website or services after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Shipping Policy.
